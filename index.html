INTRODUCTION

Hi, my name is Raul Diaz. I studied at Cal State University of San Bernardino and graduated in 2016 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. What do I do? I code. I started my programming journey at the beginning of college and it became one of my greatest decisions in life because learning how to code and understanding how the pieces of technology we use in our everyday life work is astonishing. I am glad I followed this career path and wish to continue to grow in it because being in a work field you enjoy is outstanding.

I appreciate getting my hands dirty and making things work, whether its high level or low level, if it involves learning new concepts I enjoy it. I love learning and growing my knowledge in programming, that I be finding myself tinkering with different fields, whether it's an Android application, websites with HTML/CSS, or just trying to learn or experiment with other languages.
